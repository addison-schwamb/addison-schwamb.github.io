### Research Interests
---
Clinical neuroscience presents an especially compelling domain for the engineering of digital twins, i.e., computational models that can serve as *in silico* replicas of individuals' physiology. A particular prevailing challenge in this context is the assimilation of physiological data spanning multiple spatial and temporal scales. The overall goal of my envisioned research program is to enable the development of digital twins that embed formal, transparent physiological dynamics through advances in statistical signal processing, estimation, and control.
